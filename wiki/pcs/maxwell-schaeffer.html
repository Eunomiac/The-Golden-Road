Prototype (Self-Made)

The procedure that remade him was not destroyed. It lives in notebooks, improvised schematics, and muscle memory. Given time, tools, and a willing or desperate subject, Maxwell can patiently reassemble the apparatus and invite lightning into another unshielded brain.

Effect: Maxwell can recreate the horrific procedures that birthed him. Track a prototype pool that begins at 0.

• Once per chapter in which he does not attempt to create a new Deviant, he may spend a scene preparing (examining a subject, gathering equipment, coaching breathing exercises, etc.) and roll a dice pool of 2Half Merit Value (4), rounded down to a minimum of 1 = 2. Add any successes to the prototype pool; making this roll forbids an attempt in the same chapter.

• Once per chapter, after another scene of preparation, Maxwell may roll a number of dice equal to the current prototype pool to trigger Divergence in a test subject. On a success the subject transforms, usually echoing Maxwell's own Variations and Scars; on an exceptional success the Storyteller may allow him to choose each new Variation and Scar (including setting their variables). On a failure the experiment fails, likely killing or mutilating the subject and inflicting a minor Instability on Maxwell. A dramatic failure deals a medium Instability and causes catastrophic fallout (dead subjects, fires, mobs, etc.). Any attempt to trigger Divergence resets the prototype pool to 0.

Effect: Maxwell has influence over electronics and electricity. While this Variation is active, Maxwell can affect objects in range with a Size no greater than Size 8Magnitude (2) × Scar Power (4) = 8.
