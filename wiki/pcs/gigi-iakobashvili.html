Prototype − The Red Phone

Effect: Gigi possesses The Red Phone, a detachable Prototype enhancement. Its base Variation Magnitude equals 1 (maximum 3 unless Gigi purchases 5 dots for Magnitude 4 or 7 dots for Magnitude 5). She may invest +1 additional Merit dot to restrict who can operate the Prototype (limiting it to members of a single clade or conspiracy), and +2 additional dots to assign it to the Biologic, Hypertech, or Paranormal category as appropriate. This Merit is not limited to five dots, allowing further customization and future upgrades.

Drawback: The Prototype's associated Variation or Scar must be Overt, and conspiracies everywhere covet an edge that is not shackled to an inconvenient Deviant. Expect rivals to try to steal, study, or leash it at every opportunity.

Khevsureti Spectres (Cutting Lash)

Not quite ghosts and yet not quite figments of imagination either, the spectral warriors that Gigi can draw forth from the shard of the blade lodged in her bare a striking resemblance to the warriors from Khevsureti that her parents used to tell her about so long ago. While they are able to apply an impressive amount of swordplay that can cut people down from a distance, she has to focus very hard to keep them physically present or else they threaten to evaporate. Of course, if the spirits feel they’re needed they can simply summon themselves. Unfortunately whenever these spirits are summoned voluntarily or involuntarily this restarts the effects the initial ritual had on her: making the traces of LSD in her body become much much more potent.
