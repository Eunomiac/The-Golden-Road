FIELD SYNOPSIS: Journalist whose investigative work intersected with restricted operations prior to abduction. Post-alteration, subject exhibits enhanced pattern recognition, intrusive cognitive probing capability, and compulsive information-gathering behavior. Functions as de facto analyst and interrogator for associated Renegade cluster, with growing awareness of Conspiracy methodology and infrastructure. Threat assessment: AMBER; primary risk lies in subject's ability to correlate disparate data streams and expose assets rather than in direct physical confrontation.
